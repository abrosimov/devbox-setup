## What is this seminar

The catalogue ([[../00-MoC]]) diagnoses Claude Code's behavioural problems and proposes fixes. The seminar subjects the catalogue *itself* to FPF analysis — checking whether its diagnoses are structurally sound, whether its prescriptions contradict its findings, and whether its priorities survive first-principles scrutiny.

## Next moves

Iterations 1–2 were **audits** (diagnose the catalogue); iteration 3 turned the findings
into a **constructive** artefact — LAQF formalises the 30 root causes as reusable FPF
patterns, and the attention-budget ledger pending from iteration 1 is now pattern
`LAQF.B3`. Remaining substantive moves: (a) realise the 9 Layer-2 gaps tracked in
[[12-llm-agent-quality-dpf/12-20-local-practice-edition]]; (b) field-calibrate the
measurement bands; (c) build the catalogue's own WorkPlan DAG.
